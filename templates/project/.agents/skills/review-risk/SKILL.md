# Review Risk

## Goal

Identify what could break before or after a change.

## Focus

- behavior regressions
- scope expansion
- hidden dependencies
- missing tests or verification
- rollout and deployment risk

## Output

- main risks
- affected areas
- missing checks
- recommendation before merge or handoff
